Roasted Red Pepper & Spinach Egg Bites

Recipe Information

Prep Time Cooking Time Servings
15 minutes 45 minutes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup whole milk
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup packed baby spinach, chopped
  • ⅔ cup chopped roasted red peppers

Directions

STEP 1:

Preheat oven to 177°C. Coat a 12-cup muffin tin with cooking spray.

STEP 2:

Whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper together in a large bowl until well combined and frothy. Fold in cheese, spinach and roasted red peppers until combined.

STEP 3:

Divide the mixture among the prepared muffin cups (about 1/3 cup each).

STEP 4:

Bake until puffed, set and light golden, 16 to 18 minutes. (The egg bites will deflate once removed from the oven.) Let cool in the pan for 5 minutes before serving.

Cooking Tips and Tricks:

  • Customize Your Creation: Make it your own by adding ingredients like diced tomatoes, mushrooms, or your favorite herbs and spices to suit your taste.

  • Fresh vs. Frozen: While fresh spinach adds a vibrant touch, you can also use frozen spinach. Just be sure to thaw and drain it before adding.

  • Flavor Enhancers: Experiment with different cheeses, like mozzarella or feta, and seasonings for exciting variations.

  • Don't Overcook: Avoid overbaking; a slight jiggle in the center means your egg bites will stay light and fluffy.

  • Storage Savvy: Store leftovers in the fridge or freezer for quick, delicious reheats.
