Mexican Shrimp Cocktail

Recipe Information

Prep Time Cooking Time Servings
15-20 minutes 0 minutes 4-6 servings

Ingredients

For the Cocktail Sauce:

  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon prepared horseradish (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 cup tomato sauce

For the Shrimp and Vegetables:

  • 500g large cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup diced fresh tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced avocado
  • Lime wedges for garnish
  • Tortilla chips or saltine crackers for serving(optional)

Directions

STEP 1:Prepare the Cocktail Sauce:

In a bowl, combine the ketchup, fresh lime juice, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, and horseradish (if using)

STEP 2:

Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the hot sauce to your preferred level of spiciness.

STEP 3:

Refrigerate the cocktail sauce while you prepare the other ingredients to let the flavors meld.

STEP 4:Prepare the Shrimp and Vegetables:

Thoroughly rinse the cooked shrimp under cold water to remove any ice crystals and pat them dry with a paper towel.

STEP 5:

In a large bowl, combine the diced cucumber, red onion, celery, fresh cilantro, diced tomatoes, and diced avocado.

STEP 6:

Add the cooked shrimp to the vegetable mixture.

STEP 7:Assemble the Mexican Shrimp Cocktail:

Pour the prepared cocktail sauce over the shrimp and vegetables.

STEP 8:

Gently toss everything together to coat the shrimp and vegetables evenly with the sauce.

STEP 9:Serve

Divide the Mexican shrimp cocktail into individual serving glasses or bowls,garnish with lime wedges and extra cilantro, if desired and serve with tortilla chips or saltine crackers on the side.

Cooking Tips and Tricks:

  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Whenever possible, use fresh, high-quality ingredients. Freshly cooked and peeled shrimp are ideal for this recipe.

  • Chill Ingredients: For the best flavor, chill the cooked shrimp and cocktail sauce in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving. This helps the flavors meld and makes the dish more refreshing.

  • Customize Veggies: Feel free to add or substitute vegetables according to your preference. Sliced radishes, diced bell peppers, or chopped jalape√±os can add extra flavor and crunch.

  • Devein the Shrimp:: If your cooked shrimp still have veins, consider deveining them before adding them to the cocktail. This improves the texture and appearance of the dish.

  • Make Ahead:You can prepare the cocktail sauce and chop the vegetables ahead of time. Combine everything just before serving to keep it fresh.
