Chocolate Lava Cakes

Recipe Information

Prep Time Cooking Time Servings
20 minutes 10-12 minutes 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 oz (115g) dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher), chopped
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick or 113g) unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: Cocoa powder or powdered sugar for dusting
  • Optional: Fresh berries or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for serving

Directions

STEP 1:

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

STEP 2:

Grease and lightly flour four 6-ounce ramekins or custard cups.

STEP 3:

In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the chopped dark chocolate and unsalted butter. Microwave in 20-30 second intervals, stirring after each interval until the mixture is smooth and well combined. Alternatively, you can melt the chocolate and butter over a double boiler.

STEP 4:

In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, and granulated sugar until the mixture becomes pale and slightly thickened.

STEP 5:

Pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture and gently fold them together until fully combined.

STEP 6:

Sift the all-purpose flour and a pinch of salt over the chocolate mixture. Fold until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; you want the batter to be smooth.

STEP 7:

Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins or custard cups.

STEP 8:

Place the ramekins on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes. The edges should be set, but the centers should still be soft and slightly wobbly.

STEP 9:

Carefully remove the cakes from the oven. Using oven mitts or a kitchen towel, invert each ramekin onto a serving plate. Let them sit for about 10-15 seconds, then gently lift the ramekin to reveal the molten center.

STEP 10:

Optional: Dust with cocoa powder or powdered sugar and serve with fresh berries or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Cooking Tips and Tricks:

  • Use high-quality dark chocolate with at least 70% cocoa for the best flavor.

  • Properly grease and flour the ramekins to ensure the cakes release easily.

  • Do not overbake the lava cakes; they should have a gooey, molten center.

  • You can prepare the batter in advance and refrigerate it. Just bring it to room temperature before baking.

  • Experiment with different fillings, like a caramel center or a Nutella filling, for variety.
